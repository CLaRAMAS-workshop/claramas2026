#### Review process

Submitted papers will undergo a **double-blind review** process 
involving at least **3 reviewers** per paper. 

Papers will be evaluated upon 
  * **appropriateness** to the workshop's topics
  * **novelty** with respect to the state of the art
  * technical **soundness** of the contribution
  * expected **impact** on the practical engineering of agents and MAS
  * **reproducibility** of experiments (if any)
  * **clarity** of the exposition
 
Reviewers will have the chance to accept a paper as a **poster** 
instead of a full paper, *provided that the authors opted in* during submission. 

In that case, a shortened, **3-page** (excluding references) extended abstract version 
will be accepted as accompanying the poster.
