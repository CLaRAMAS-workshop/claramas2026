📢 The Organising Committee of CLaRAMAS 
announces an **exceptional deadline extension** to papers (full, short, posters) submission 
to **welcome authors of papers rejected by [CLeaR 2026](https://www.cclear.cc/2026/)**: 
the new, last, stricter-than-ever deadline is **March, 11th 2026** 📢

This leaves CLeaR authors roughly 2 days to make changes and submit. 
Considering that AAMAS eary registration is available until March 31st, 
this is the most we can do to avoid squeezing even further the already tight review period. 

![image-center]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/img/deadline_extension.png){: .align-center}

*No more excuses* not to send your amazing research works to the workshop 😉

Check the [Call for Papers](/claramas2026/cfp) for the topics of interest 
and the 🗳️ [submission page](/claramas2026/submission) for the submission instructions 😉
