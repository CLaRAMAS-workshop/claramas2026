Dear everybody, 
🙏 thank 🙏 you very much for attending this successful 1st edition of CLaRAMAS :)

In order for this event not to be an isolated chance, 
but a first step in a community journey, 
we kindly ask you if you are interested in staying in the "CLaRAMAS loop", 
by filling [this short form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScQegfTuVVmzD-tTa7Yw1drRc12fooT4-HD-zjDwK5vfHuBZA/viewform?usp=header) ✍🏻. 

By doing so, you:
  - ✅ will be contacted for the next editions of CLaRAMAS, and other related events
  - ✅ will be invited to submit your work to the CLaRAMAS post-proceedings (even if you had no submission to the workshop!)
  - ✅ will help shape this afternoon's focus groups :)

Please, 🤝 **help us build the CLaRAMAS community 
to support research collaboration opportunities!** 🤜🤛
